What does it mean to be and Entrepreneur?.

An entrepreneur is someone who recognizes opportunities, takes risks, and works to create value by developing innovative solutions, often through starting or managing a business. They are proactive, resourceful, and take on the responsibility of solving problems while striving to achieve their vision and goals.
